Commands

All commands begin with >>

8ball question
Find the answers you seek.
coinflip
Heads or Tails!
dab
Dab on all your haters.
delete [num]
Deletes messages in the channel. Note: Permission locked to "Manage Messages".
diceroll [num]
Returns a number from 1 to number specified.
image [search term]
Searches google images and returns a random image based on the search term.
insult
Randomly generated insult towards others (>>insult @user) or (>>insult) yourself.
say [message]
Red Queen shall repeat you.
play [search term or youtube url]
Queues song and joins current VC (if not already present). Note: Must be in a voice channel.
queue
Gets list of upcoming videos in server queue.
skip
Skips current video in queue and proceeds to next, if any.
stop
Ends music playback and leaves current voice channel.
shuffle
Shuffles the playback queue.
nowplaying
Find out what is currently playing in the voice channel.
youtube [search term]
Searches youtube and returns the first result.
